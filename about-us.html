About Us

Zoe Construction


Zoe Construction is a Zimbabwean company formulated to solve some of the country’s toughest construction problems. Our team of qualified professionals is dedicated to providing quality services in the fields of Town Planning and Civil Engineering. Our top priority to you as our clients is to provide unquestionable quality services and impart market knowledge on the value of your property and how to add value to the property. We offer diverse construction solutions to everyday Zimbabweans, small institutions and large organisations. We range from residential, commercial to industrial buildings offering property consultation and contracting services balancing quality service and affordable rates.
